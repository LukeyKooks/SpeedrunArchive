TIPS & TECHNIQUES

General Strategy

  • Stay calm under pressure — panic leads to mistakes
  • Learn each game’s map before serious runs
  • Memorise enemy behaviour and spawn patterns
  • Use practice runs to isolate difficult sections
Consistency matters more than raw speed early on.

Speedrunning Tips

  • Optimise routes to reduce downtime
  • Use consistent keybinds and sensitivity
  • Watch top runners for advanced techniques
  • Reset early if the run is already behind pace
  • Record attempts to review mistakes

Game Sense

Good speedrunners don’t just react — they predict. Understanding AI behaviour, timing windows, and resource cycles gives a major advantage.

In horror games like FNAF, audio cues are often more important than visuals.

Advanced Techniques

  • Frame-perfect inputs (where applicable)
  • Momentum control and movement cancelling
  • Exploiting AI pathing limitations
  • Route skipping and sequence breaking
  • Safe glitch usage to avoid soft-locks

Boneworks Tips

Physics mastery is the foundation of Boneworks speedrunning. Momentum and object interaction define every strategy.

  • Practice prop-flying using small objects
  • Use body-launching to skip sections
  • Chain jumps to maintain momentum
  • Avoid unnecessary collisions
  • Skip enemies whenever possible

Minecraft Tips

Minecraft speedrunning is heavily RNG-based. Fast resets and efficient routing are essential.

  • Practice sprint-jumping movement
  • Use hotkeys for fast crafting
  • Reset for good world seeds quickly
  • Master Nether routing and fortresses
  • Efficiently trade with Piglins for pearls
If blaze RNG is bad early, reset — saving time is more important than forcing a run.

FNAF: Security Breach Tips

  • Use slide-boosting and bunny hopping for speed
  • Learn consistent wall-clip locations
  • Skip missions using early triggers when possible
  • Exploit geometry for out-of-bounds movement
  • Use movement over stealth whenever possible
Animatronics can often be outrun — hesitation is usually what gets runs killed.

Final Insight

Speedrunning success comes from repetition, patience, and understanding systems deeply. Every mistake is a learning opportunity, and consistency always beats raw talent over time.
